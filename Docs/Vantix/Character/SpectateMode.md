# SpectateMode

`Vantix.Character.SpectateMode`

Spectator camera mode for a `PuppetPlayer`: no camera, third-person follow, or first-person through the puppet's eyes.

## Fields

| Name | Summary |
|------|---------|
| `Fps` | First-person — sees through the puppet's eyes without a viewmodel. |
| `None` | Default — no camera active; the puppet is rendered in the world. |
| `Tps` | Follow camera — third-person camera enabled. |
